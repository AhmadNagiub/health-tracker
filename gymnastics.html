10 tips for exercising safely
● Take five to 10 minutes to warm up and cool down properly.
● Plan to start slowly and boost your activity level gradually.
● Be aware that training too hard.
● Listen to your body. Hold off on exercise when you're sick or feeling very fatigued.
● If you stop exercising for a while, drop back to a lower level of exercise initially.
● For most people, simply drinking plenty of water is sufficient.
● Choose clothes and shoes designed for your type of exercise.
● For strength training, good form is essential.
● Exercising vigorously in hot, humid conditions can lead to serious overheating and dehydration. Slow pace when the temperature is above 70°F.
● Dress properly for cold-weather workouts to avoid hypothermia.
